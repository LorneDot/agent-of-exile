# PoE2 Unique Item Synergies

Reference for common unique item synergies and build-around patterns.

## Common Synergy Patterns

### Attribute Stacking
- **Enablers**: Astramentis, Pillar of the Caged God
- **Ascendancy**: Gemling Legionnaire (double attribute bonuses)
- **Scaling**: Stack STR/DEX/INT on all gear slots
- **Payoff**: Pillar grants AoE per INT, attack speed per DEX, weapon damage per STR

### Elemental Ailment Conversion
- **Enablers**: The Three Dragons, The Taming
- **Pattern**: Fire shocks, Cold ignites, Lightning freezes
- **Ascendancies**: Stormweaver, Infernalist, Deadeye
- **Synergy**: Stack one damage type but get all ailment benefits

### Flask Automation
- **Enablers**: Mageblood
- **Ascendancy**: Pathfinder (flask effect scaling)
- **Pattern**: Permanent magic utility flasks with 70-100% increased effect
- **Payoff**: Massive defense/offense from flasks with zero management

### Physical Mitigation
- **Enablers**: Lightning Coil (phys taken as lightning)
- **Pattern**: Stack max lightning res (Purity of Lightning, +max res on tree)
- **Payoff**: Physical hits converted to elemental at 75-90% resistance

### Mana Stacking
- **Enablers**: Atziri's Foible, Archmage support
- **Ascendancy**: Stormweaver
- **Pattern**: Stack INT + mana, scale damage off mana pool
- **Payoff**: Massive flat lightning damage added to spells

### Charge Stacking
- **Enablers**: Ralakesh's Impatience
- **Pattern**: Always have max charges without generating them
- **Ascendancy**: Gemling Legionnaire (charge bonuses)
- **Payoff**: Permanent charge bonuses without charge generation investment

## When to Use Uniques vs Rares

### Use Uniques For:
- Build-enabling mechanics (cannot get elsewhere)
- Tying together a build's core concept
- Filling slots where rare mod pools are weak

### Use Rares For:
- Life and resistances (uniques typically lack these)
- Flexible mod combinations tailored to your gaps
- Incremental power upgrades (crafting/trading)

### Rule of Thumb:
- 1-2 build-enabling uniques max
- Rest rares with life + resists
- Never sacrifice life/resists on 3+ slots for unique effects
